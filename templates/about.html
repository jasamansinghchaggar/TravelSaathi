About TravelSaathi

Revolutionizing travel safety through innovation and technology

Our Mission

At TravelSaathi, we're committed to transforming the travel experience by making it safer, smarter, and more enjoyable. Our mission is to leverage cutting-edge technology to protect travelers and provide them with tools that enhance their journey.

We believe that everyone deserves to travel with peace of mind, knowing they have a reliable companion looking out for their safety and comfort every step of the way.

Our Vision

We envision a world where travel-related accidents and emergencies are minimized through preventive technology. TravelSaathi aims to be the global leader in travel safety technology, creating a network of protected travelers who can explore the world with confidence.

By combining artificial intelligence, real-time data analysis, and community-driven information sharing, we're building the future of travel safety today.

Our Story

TravelSaathi was born out of a hackathon idea, where a group of passionate developers and travel enthusiasts came together to address the growing concerns around travel safety. What started as a project to create a simple drowsiness detection system has evolved into a comprehensive travel companion application.

After witnessing the alarming statistics of road accidents caused by fatigue and lack of proper assistance during breakdowns, our team was determined to create a solution that could make a real difference in people's lives.

Today, TravelSaathi combines various safety features including AI-powered drowsiness detection, mechanic assistance, emergency SOS, and road condition reporting to create the ultimate travel companion application.
